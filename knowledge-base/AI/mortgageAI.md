### Can I get a mortgage for off-plan property?
Yes, but it is limited.

### What is the maximum loan-to-value (LTV) for off-plan?
- Maximum: 50%

Source: https://www.centralbank.ae/

### What does 50% LTV mean?
You must:
- Pay at least 50% upfront
- Fund the rest via mortgage or payment plan

### How does this compare to completed property?
| Property Type | LTV |
|--------------|-----|
| Completed | up to 80% |
| Off-plan | 50% |

### Why is off-plan harder to finance?
- Higher risk for banks
- Construction uncertainty
- No completed asset as collateral

### Do developers offer alternatives to mortgages?
Yes:
- Post-handover payment plans
- Installment-based payment schedules

### Are there protections for buyers using financing?
In Abu Dhabi:
- Developer financing must go into escrow
- Buyers must be informed of funding structure

Source: https://adrec.gov.ae/

### Can I still qualify for a Golden Visa with a mortgage?
Yes, if:
- You meet AED 2M threshold
- You provide bank NOC

### What documents are required for approval?
Not specified in official sources. Typically determined by lenders.

### Key takeaway for investors
Off-plan investing requires:
- High upfront capital
- Limited leverage
