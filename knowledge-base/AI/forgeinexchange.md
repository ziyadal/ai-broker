### Are there foreign exchange controls in the UAE?
No. The UAE has no foreign exchange controls.

Source: https://u.ae/en/information-and-services/business/

### Can I move money freely in and out of the UAE?
Yes. You can:
- Bring funds into UAE
- Repatriate profits abroad

### Can I send property sale profits abroad?
Yes, generally allowed.

### How do I exchange currency for property transactions?
Through:
- Banks
- Licensed exchange houses

### Are there compliance checks when transferring money?
Yes. Transactions are subject to AML regulations.

Source: https://mun.rak.ae/

### What might I need to provide?
- Source of funds
- Identity verification
- Transaction justification

### Are there limits on transferring money abroad?
No explicit limits, but:
- Banks may apply compliance checks

### Key takeaway for investors
- UAE is highly capital-friendly
- Expect AML checks for large transactions
