# UAE Golden Visa — Real Estate Investor Route

## Overview
If you own a property (or group of properties) in the UAE, you may be eligible for a Golden Visa. Mortgaged properties may be accepted.

## Eligibility

### Who qualifies
Real estate investors who own a property or a group of properties in the UAE.

### Minimum investment threshold
- The total value of owned property/properties must be **at least AED 2,000,000**.
- If ownership is a **share in a jointly owned property**, the value of the applicant’s share must be **at least AED 2,000,000**.

## Requirements

### Proof of ownership / valuation
- Provide a **property status statement / ownership letter** issued by the relevant emirate’s land department (e.g., Dubai Land Department).
- Property value must be certified by the land department.
- A property valuation certificate may also be accepted if issued through **valuation offices licensed** by the Dubai Land Department.

### Mortgaged property rules
- Purchasing a property with a **loan/mortgage** may be acceptable if the loan is from **specific local banks approved by the competent local entity**.

### Lien requirement
- A **lien** may be required on the property to ensure continued ownership throughout the Golden Residency validity (per land department procedures).

## Processing time
- Application completion time: **5 days**

## Key conditions to maintain the visa
- One condition for maintaining the Golden Residence Permit is being able to **support yourself and your family** without requiring government support.
- The authority may take measures to ensure individuals continue to meet the conditions throughout the visa validity.
- During the residency period, the property may not be disposed of (and any financial deposit may not be refunded) for the required duration, as applicable under the rules.

## Benefits

### Family sponsorship
- Ability to sponsor **family members**, including spouses and children **regardless of age**.

### Domestic helpers
- Ability to sponsor an **unlimited number of domestic helpers**.

### If the primary holder dies
- Family members can remain in the UAE **until the end of their permit duration** if the primary Golden Visa holder passes away.

### 180-day rule exception
- Golden Residence Permit holders are **exempt from the 180-day residency rule** (residency becomes void only if it expires while the holder is outside the UAE).
