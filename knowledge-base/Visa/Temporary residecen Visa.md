# UAE Real Estate Residence Visa

## Overview
A foreigner who owns real estate in the UAE can obtain a residence visa without requiring employment.

The individual is allowed to remain in the country for **up to 60 days** from the date of entry while completing the necessary residence issuance procedures.

---

## Fees

### Base Fees
- Service fee: **AED 1,100**
- Work visa fee: **AED 200**
- Value-added tax: **5%**

### Additional Fees (if sponsored person is inside the UAE)
- Knowledge Dirham: **AED 10**
- Innovation Dirham: **AED 10**
- Inside country fee: **AED 500**
